Major Projects

Final Year Project

Investigated the efficacy of Aerogel as an insulator for superconductors during magnetic levitation. Designed a primitive cryostat.

  • Compared theory, simulation, and experimental results
  • Focused on magnetostatic and thermal simulations
  • Sourced superconductors, aerogel, and magnets on a £75 budget
  • Negotiated with Cambridge University and suppliers
  • Final report awarded First Class (71%)

RAZ Robot (Robotic Autonomous Zimmerframe)

Started as a competition, later became a £6000 funded university group project to assist elderly mobility.

  • Led the entire project with minimal staff involvement

  • Individual Subprojects included:
