Major Projects

Final Year Project

Investigated the efficacy of using Aerogel as an insulator for superconductors during magnetic levitation and designed a primitive cryostat.

  • Compared theory to simulated and experimental data
  • Focused on magnetostatic and thermal simulations of the superconductor
  • Sourced rare components including aerogel, superconductors, and large magnets
  • Worked with a strict £75 budget and negotiated with Cambridge University and suppliers
  • Final report received a First Class mark of 71%

RAZ Robot (Robotic Autonomous Zimmerframe)

Began as a design competition; became a university-funded £6000 project led by me to assist the elderly with mobility.

  • Led the project with minimal university oversight
  • Directed proposal writing, concept design, and system development
  • Managed budget, procurement, and recruitment (including design competition for a product designer)
  • Oversaw subprojects including:
    • Lifting mechanism design
    • Arm pressure matrix using Velostat
    • Planar movement system for the robot
